Privacy Policy for ChatGPT Citation Generator

Last updated: 29.12.2023

1. Introduction

The ChatGPT Citation Generator is a browser extension designed to create BibTeX citations from ChatGPT conversations. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle data related to this extension.

2. Data Collection and Usage

The ChatGPT Citation Generator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The extension operates solely within your browser, accessing only the content of the ChatGPT interface in the active tab for citation generation. No information is shared with external servers or third parties.

3. Permissions

The ChatGPT Citation Generator operates using content scripts that are limited to the domain "chat.openai.com". The extension requires permission to inject these scripts into the ChatGPT interface pages for its functionality. These scripts are used exclusively for generating citations from the ChatGPT conversation content and do not track the user's browsing history or access data on other websites. No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted by the extension.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
